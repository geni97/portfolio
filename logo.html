Logo Redesign

My redesigned version of the Pure Gelato logo. Pure Gelato is a local shop that sells wonderful gelato. It needed a logo that could represent exactly how great it is. So I designed one. Fresh colours, simple design, inviting but modern. Exactly like the store itself, and exactly like the product, of course!
