Send Me Wildflowers

A line illustration drawn first by hand and then digitally. My line illustrations are created to have a hand-drawn look and feel, with lines creating all the shapes and textures. The colours used in Send Me Wildflowers are muted tones, and are applied with a simple, solid look allowing the lines to do all the work.
